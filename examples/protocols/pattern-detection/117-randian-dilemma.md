## Core Definition

> **Randian Dilemma**: Everyone knows the truth (L1), but no one can legally prove it (L4 blocks), so everyone performs the fiction (L2), and truth-speakers are punished (L3) = Stable bad equilibrium.

This is NOT individual hypocrisy. It's structural, collective, and self-reinforcing.

## Four-Layer Architecture

| Layer | Name | Content | Function |
|-------|------|---------|----------|
| **L1** | Truth | Observable reality, statistical patterns (95%+ confidence) | What everyone privately knows |
| **L2** | Fiction | Official narrative, plausible deniability | Social performance, legally defensible lies |
| **L3** | Punishment | Career destruction, legal prosecution, social exile | Enforcement mechanism (keeps people in L2) |
| **L4** | Mechanism | Structural designs that make L1 legally unprovable | Enables L1-L3 gap to persist |
