## 2. The fulfillment Cycle (Monthly)

### Week 1: Audit & Strategy (The "Senior" Work)

- **Run**: Technical Audit (Screaming Frog / Ahrefs Free).
- **Run**: Competitor Gap Analysis (Semrush).
- **Deliver**: "The battle plan" (PDF). *Agencies charge setup fees for this; you do it to prove competence.*

### Week 2: Content Production (The "Factory")

- **Input**: 4x Keywords (e.g., "Best SEO Agency Singapore", "Corporate Secretarial Services").
- **Process**:
  1. Generate Outline (Competitor H2 analysis).
  2. Draft Content (Claude/Gemini - "Human/Bionic" voice).
  3. Generate Graphics (Charts/Diagrams, not stock photos).
- **Output**: 4x Google Docs ready for upload.

### Week 3: On-Page & Technical Fixes

- **Action**: Log into CMS (WordPress/Webflow).
- **Fix**: Titles, Metas, Alt Tags, Schema Markup (FAQ Schema, LocalBusiness Schema).
- **Speed**: Verify Core Web Vitals (Green scores = happy clients).

### Week 4: Link Injection & Reporting

- **Buy**: Purchase 1-2 Guest Posts or Niche Edits (Vendor List).
- **Report**: Send automated report (Traffic, Rankings, Conversions).
- **Touchpoint**: 15-min Loom video walking through the "wins".

## 3. The "Secret Sauce" (Differentiation)

The "Mustard" example claims "Conversion Focused SEO" vs "Traffic".
**How to fake this**:

1. **Money Pages First**: Don't blog about random tips. Build "Service Pages" (e.g., `/singapore-seo-services`).

3. **CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization)**: Add "Sticky CTAs" and "Calendly Embeds" to every page.
